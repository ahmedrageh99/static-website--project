EU / UK Responsible Person (RP)

Appoint a trusted partner to act as your official contact for market authorities while keeping your brand in control of key decisions.

  • Designation as EU and/or UK Responsible Person for your products.
  • Notification of products on the relevant cosmetics portals.
  • Verification that PIF, safety data, and artwork are in place and accessible.
  • Regulatory correspondence and support during inspections or queries.

Who we are

About Hamdah Consulting Unit

Hamdah Consulting Unit was created to support indie and emerging cosmetic brands that need clear, accessible guidance on complex regulatory rules. Many founders feel lost between chemistry, safety data, and legal language. We exist to translate those requirements into practical steps and actionable roadmaps.

Drawing on experience with EU/UK cosmetic regulations, quality systems, and audit-ready documentation, our team focuses on giving you clarity: what to do, in what order, and which documents you need in place before launch.

How we work with you

  • Start with a discovery call to understand your formulas, markets, and launch dates.
  • Design a step-by-step compliance plan tailored to your product portfolio.

Values that guide our approach

  • Clarity: We avoid legal jargon and explain the “why” behind each requirement.
  • Integrity: We prioritise consumer safety and truthful marketing claims.
  • Partnership: We collaborate with your internal and external labs, designers, and suppliers.
  • Scalability: We design documentation systems that grow with your brand.
